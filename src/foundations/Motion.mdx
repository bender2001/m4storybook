# Motion

M3 Expressive easings (`emphasized`, `standard`, ±decel/accel) and
durations (short1..long4) live in `src/tokens/motion.ts` and surface
as Tailwind transitions and `motion/react` presets. Spring presets
(`springy`, `gentle`, `snappy`) are available from `@/motion`.
